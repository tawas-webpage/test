WHY DO I LIKE FLIES SO MUCH?

I've gotten this question countless times. So many of my peers ridicule me for my hyperfixation with flies. I am aware that plenty of people have convictions that contradict mine, however I will not forcefully argue the point


One of the crucial aspects of my love for flies is their vast variety of abilities. Although they are small, they still possess the ability to fly, feed, have about 700,000 eyes (I chose that number arbitrarily), and there is scientific proof that they see in slow-motion
